Save Our Stations!

Recently 10 fire stations were closed. We must re-open them to keep our city safe!

Join our peaceful protest on January 10th 2016 and demand the re-opening of our essential fire stations.

Time is important!

Every second counts when it comes to saving lives. We need enough fire stations to be open so they can help us quickly in our time of need.

In just 1 year, the time it takes for fire engines to reach those in need has increased to almost 9 minutes! That's up by almost 50% compared to the year before.

Why should we worry? Well according to Fire Engineering, if those in need don't get support within 10 minutes they'll almost certainly die!

You are important!

We're proud to be Londoners, and we're growing... Fast!

Now there are more than 8.5 million people that call London home. Yet, in the past year alone, the Goverment has closed 10 fire stations in London.

Those stations have disappeared, but the people have not!
